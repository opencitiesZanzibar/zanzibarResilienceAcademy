This product is intends to showcase the 3 main outputs of the Zanzibar Resilience Academy. They include a drainage map, a base map and a Household survey of Zanzibar Urban West. These were created by over 50 students who participated in a 2 month industrial training programme that mapped 42 Shehias within Zanzibar Urban West. This activity was run by Spatial Collective and facilitated by the State University of Zanzibar.

The main goal of the industrial training was to teach the students about practical and technical aspects of mapping with the goal of generating open data to fulfil geo-data needs of the island. The Resilience Academy is a multi-university initiative that aims to equip university students with the tools, knowledge, and skills to address their most pressing urban challenges and ensure resilient urban development.
