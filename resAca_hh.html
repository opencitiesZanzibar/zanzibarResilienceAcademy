Zanzibar Urban West experiences cases of flooding normally caused by periods of intense rainfall. From a stakeholder engagement, we identified flooding hotspots within Urban West and students conducted a household survey of 2300 households in one of these hotspot areas.
This area has a water body surrounded by a built up area. We selected households within a 400 metre buffer around this water body. Data collected included characteristics of the buildings, access to water, electricity and sanitation and whether the building occupants have experienced flooding in their specific household or within the area that they live.
